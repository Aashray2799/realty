Property Management Services

Satya Realty is a FULL SERVICE Property Management Company dedicated to providing excellent service to our Owners and Tenants of quality Single Family Homes, Townhomes and Condominiums. If you need to rent out a house or other residential property, we can take care of everything! As a member of the National Association of Residential Property Managers, our team is ready to manage your properties with the highest level of professionalism, experience and service. Whether you are a homeowner or investor looking to take advantage of today’s real estate marketplace, our experience in real estate sales, rentals and management services allows us the unique ability to provide valuable insight and services which are designed to maximize your property’s overall profitability and return. Our Management Services include: Initial on-site property inspection Establish fair rental market value Advertising the property “FOR RENT” using both print and digital properties including MLS, Zillow, Realtor.com, Homes.com, and many others. Meeting prospective tenant(s) at the property for showings Processing of rental application (Credit Check, Employment Verification, Rental History Verification) Screening of applicant(s) (Criminal and Background Check) Prepare North Carolina Residential Rental Contract and applicable addendums Move-in inspection with tenant(s) Tenant(s) quality control Online Rental Payment Portal Filing of legal proceedings for breach of lease agreement Preparation of monthly and yearly accounting to owners Respond to both non-emergency maintenance calls For emergency maintenance, we offer a 24-hour emergency answering service Move-out inspection with tenant(s) Our goal is to take away the headache of being a landlord and allowing you to focus on real estate investing without the having to manage rental property.
