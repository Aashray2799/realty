About Us

Hello! I’m Satya Vardireddy, and I’m thrilled to welcome you to Satya Realty LLC here in Charlotte, NC. With over three years of experience in the real estate industry, I’ve had the privilege of helping clients buy and sell a variety of properties, including houses, multi-family units, townhouses, condos, and land. Throughout my career, I’ve successfully closed 29 transactions with a total value of $14.6 million. My expertise spans a broad price range, from $160K to $901K, with an average sale price of $503.1K. Whether you’re looking to buy your dream home or sell a property, my goal is to provide you with exceptional service and guidance every step of the way.
